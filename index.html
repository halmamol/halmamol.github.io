Hello, I'm Helena!

I am a neutrino physicist with over 8 years of research experience in academia where proudly contributed in 3 different collaborations. My expertise spans Gd loaded liquid scintillator experiments, noble gas time projection chamber technologies, and water cherenkov detectors. I worked on 4 institutions across the world, showcasing my adaptability and readiness to tackle challenging situations. Enthusiastic, self-motivated and hardworking, with strong communication skills in both spoken and written forms, I excel at achieve targets and working under pressure.

My Research

I've carried out neutrino studies in 4 different institutions (MPIK - Germany, DIPC - Spain, Harvard Universtiy - USA, The University of Manchester - UK).

  • Double Chooz/STEREO

    MPhys and PhD in reactor neutrino experiments. Thesis was focused of the study of neutron detection efficiency of both detectors.

  • NEXT

    PDRA and Research Fellow in a neutrinoless double beta decay detector. Working on data analysis and simulations, together with assembly and run coordinator. detector.

  • WCTE/Hyper-K

    Research Fellow at Water Cherenkov Detectors. Currently getting involved on the physics of the WCTE and Hyper-K experiments.
